Poetry

Golden rays spill through the skies, Waking dreams in sleepy eyes. The world begins a brand new song, A place where weary hearts belong.🌻

The breeze hums secrets in the trees, A lullaby upon the seas. Mountains stand like ancient kings, Guarding earth with silent wings.🌿

A single glance, a spark ignites, Two hearts drawn close on quiet nights. No need for words, no need to speak, In love, it's soul to soul we seek.💖

Wander not with steps confined, But with a curious, daring mind. Chase the stars and forge your fate, For dreams will knock if you don’t wait.🌌

Behind the smile, a storm may cry, Clouded thoughts drift through the sky. But even rain, in time, must cease, And leave behind a breath of peace.🌦️

Stillness blooms where chaos fades, In quiet hearts, the noise obeys. Not all who pause have lost their way, Some choose the calm to light their day.🕊️

Fall you might, but rise you must, From broken dreams, rebuild your trust. Ashes hold the strongest flame, A phoenix never leaves the same.🐦‍🔥
